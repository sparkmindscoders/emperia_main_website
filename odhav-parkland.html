About Odhav Parkland Description

Odhav Parkland, a project by Setu Landmarks is built with a primary aim to improve the much-affected lifestyle of people due to escalating pollution. There is a significant reduction in the green cover as well due to commercialization. This township brings you back to the lap of nature with its facilities like gazebo sitting, theme-based garden and yoga & meditation center, Indoor-outdoor game facilities, clubhouse, and a children's park ensuring that you stay fit and healthy.
